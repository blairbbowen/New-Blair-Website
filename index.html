    Treat Selections at Swig

    The treat selections at Swig are the lowest on the totem poll. the drinks are the best, they have only a few cookie options
    and the pretzel bites are not ideal. do however have a yummy pink frosted sugar cookie, which would be my only
    reccomendation if you go there for a treat and a drink.

    Treat Selections at Thirst

    The treat selections at Thirst are the best of all. They have delicious Scotcharoos, Beignets, and the best parmesen pretzel bites.

    Treat Selections at Sodalicious

    The cookies are the way to go here. Sodalicious has the superior cookie, and they do a seasonal one as well!
    Some favorites of mine have been the Caramel one, and the Peppermint one at Christmas.
    Peanut Butter Off The Rails is also the best!
